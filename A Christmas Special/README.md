Download the entire directory for the full adventure.

The story exists inside the docx file while the image files are there for monster stats and useful props.

This story will require a lot of logical thinking from your party to successfully navigate what seems to be a never ending torture puzzle.

As DM you can use your discretion to make this as difficult or easy as you fancy.

My family loved it. We played it just before new years, sat around the table in "the dungeon" while the adults had tankards of ale and the kids drank fizz.

Lights down low and find some atmospheric music and everyone really drifts away into the story.
